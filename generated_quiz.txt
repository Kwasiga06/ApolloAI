Question 1: A projectile is launched at an angle of 45° above the horizontal with an initial speed of 20 m/s. What is the maximum height reached by the projectile? (Assume g = 10 m/s²)
A) 10 m
B) 20 m
C) 5 m
D) 15 m
Answer: A

Question 2: A 2 kg block is being pulled across a horizontal surface with a coefficient of kinetic friction μₖ = 0.3 by a force of 10 N applied at an angle of 30° above the horizontal. What is the acceleration of the block? (g = 10 m/s²)
A) 1.8 m/s²
B) 2.2 m/s²
C) 2.8 m/s²
D) 3.2 m/s²
Answer: B

Question 3: A wheel with moment of inertia I = 0.5 kg·m² is rotating at 4 rad/s. A constant torque of 2 N·m is applied for 3 seconds. What is the final angular velocity of the wheel?
A) 12 rad/s
B) 16 rad/s
C) 10 rad/s
D) 8 rad/s
Answer: B

Question 4: In a perfectly inelastic collision, a 3 kg object moving at 8 m/s collides with a 2 kg object at rest. What is the velocity of the combined objects after collision?
A) 4.8 m/s
B) 5.2 m/s
C) 3.6 m/s
D) 6.4 m/s
Answer: A

Question 5: An ideal gas undergoes an isothermal process where its volume doubles. If the initial pressure is 2 atm, what is the final pressure?
A) 4 atm
B) 2 atm
C) 1 atm
D) 0.5 atm
Answer: C
